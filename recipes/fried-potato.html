Fried potato recipe

Fried potato recipe procedures

This is made with highly garnished and nutritious potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Groundnut oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Add the seasonings into the groundnut oil
  3. Fry the potatoes inside the groundnut oil
  4. Put the ketchup in a serving bowl
